Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about SSAG and our systems.

Do I need a lot of money to start?

No. Most SSAG systems are designed to be started with very low overhead. Many of them can be launched using tools you already have or free platforms.

Can beginners use these systems?

Yes. Everything is written in plain language and structured step-by-step. You don’t need to be “business savvy” to follow the plan.

How fast can I make money?

That depends on your action and your market. Most systems are built around a 30-day launch plan so you can get into motion quickly.

Are these just eBooks?

No. Each kit is a full system with contracts, scripts, pricing, checklists, operations guides, and marketing assets—not just information.

What if I want more help?

That’s what the SSAG Partner Program and Done-For-You builds are for. You can plug in at the level of support you need right now.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. You can always start with a single kit and later add the bundle, join the Partner Program, or move into a Done-For-You build.
